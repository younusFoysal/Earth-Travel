Our Popular Tours

The Chittagong Hill Tracts Tour unveils the breathtaking landscapes and diverse cultures of southeastern Bangladesh.

  • Explore the tranquility of Kaptai Lake nestled amid lush hills, offering a picturesque escape in southeastern Bangladesh.
  • Trekking Adventures: Embark on trekking adventures to witness the natural grandeur of the area, from hill points to mesmerizing tribal villages.
  • Tribal Bazaar Exploration: Immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of tribal bazaars, where fish, meat, vegetables, and local products come to life.

The journey winds through tribal villages, and scenic viewpoints, creating a rich tapestry of ethnic diversity. Each tribe has its distinctive rites, rituals, dress, language, and cultural patterns. Their own weaving methods, tribal floating markets & bazaars are noticeable.
